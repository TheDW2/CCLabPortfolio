Description Given

Small wooden table with one wooden chair

Napkin atop the table

Mug on top of the napkin

Outdoors

Nice, sunny day

Interpretation

I imagined that this place is some sort of outdoor seating for a cafe. I put the table on a sidewalk, near the curb to give some depth.

To further show that it's outdoors on a nice day, I made a blue sky, a single cloud, and the sun.

I also made some steam coming out of the mug to show that it has a hot liquid inside.

I gave the table some wood grain to show that the table is wood beyond just its brown color.

Reflection

At several points in the assignment, I realized that just color or shape wasn't enough to precisely convey aspects of the environment. For example, I originally just made the table brown to show that it was wood, but it didn't communicate the texture of wood. It could just as easily have been just a brown table, which led me to giving the table a wood grain.

Another example would be the curb. I decided to split the curb in half to break it up rather than just having a gray block which didn't read properly as concrete.
